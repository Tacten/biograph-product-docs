# Key Features and Benefits

## Patient Care
- **Patient Registration** with comprehensive demographics and medical history
- **Duplicate detection** to prevent duplicate patient records
- **Patient Portal** for self-service access to appointments and records

## Appointments & Scheduling
- **Flexible scheduling** with practitioner availability management
- **Multiple appointment types** with configurable durations and charges
- **Automated reminders** to reduce no-shows
- **Teleconsultation support** for remote care

## Clinical Workflows
- **Patient Encounters** for structured clinical documentation
- **Vital Signs** recording with standard medical parameters
- **Prescriptions** for medications, lab tests, procedures, and therapies
- **Medical coding** with ICD-10 and SNOMED support
- **Clinical Notes** for detailed documentation

## Diagnostics & Laboratory
- **Configurable lab test templates** with normal ranges
- **Sample collection tracking** from collection to result
- **Observation-based results** following FHIR standards
- **Diagnostic report generation** with custom print formats

## Inpatient Management
- **Admission, Transfer, Discharge (ATD)** workflow
- **Bed and occupancy management** with real-time tracking
- **Inpatient medication ordering** and administration tracking
- **Discharge summaries** with comprehensive care documentation

## Billing & Insurance
- **Integrated billing** linked to appointments, encounters, and procedures
- **Healthcare packages** for bundled services
- **Insurance claim management** with payor contracts and eligibility
- **Payment tracking** with multiple payment method support

## Rehabilitation
- **Therapy planning** with customizable therapy types
- **Session scheduling** and progress tracking
- **Exercise management** with difficulty levels
- **Patient assessments** for rehabilitation progress
