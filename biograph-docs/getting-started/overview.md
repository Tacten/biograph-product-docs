# What is Biograph

Biograph is a modern, open-source **Healthcare Information System (HIS)** designed to digitize and streamline healthcare operations for clinics, hospitals, and multi-specialty healthcare facilities. Built on the proven Frappe and ERPNext platform, Biograph provides a comprehensive suite of tools that covers the entire patient care journey — from registration and appointment scheduling through clinical consultation, diagnostics, inpatient care, billing, and insurance management.

Biograph follows **HL7 FHIR** (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources) standards, ensuring your healthcare data is structured in globally recognized formats that support interoperability with other health systems.

## Why Biograph?

- **All-in-one platform** — Patient management, clinical workflows, billing, pharmacy, laboratory, and insurance in a single integrated system
- **No vendor lock-in** — Open-source under GNU GPL v3, giving you full control over your data and customizations
- **Built on ERPNext** — Leverages a mature ERP system for accounting, HR, inventory, and supply chain, so your healthcare system works seamlessly with back-office operations
- **Standards-compliant** — HL7 FHIR-based data models for interoperability
- **Regional support** — India-specific integrations including ABDM (Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission)
