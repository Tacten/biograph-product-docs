# Financial Reports

Biograph provides healthcare-specific financial reporting through ERPNext.

## Available Reports

| Report | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Medication Item Wise Sales** | Revenue breakdown by medication |
| **Patient Appointment Analytics** | Appointment volume and revenue trends |
| **Service-wise Revenue** | Revenue by service type (consultation, lab, procedure, etc.) |

## Standard ERPNext Financial Reports

All standard ERPNext accounting reports are available:
- **Profit & Loss** — Revenue and expenses
- **Balance Sheet** — Assets and liabilities
- **Accounts Receivable** — Outstanding patient payments
- **General Ledger** — Detailed transaction log
- **Gross Profit** — Margin analysis by service/item

> **Tip:** Use ERPNext's report builder to create custom healthcare financial reports based on your facility's specific needs.
