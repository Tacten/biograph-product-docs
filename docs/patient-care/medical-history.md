# Medical History Management

Biograph maintains a comprehensive medical history for each patient. This information is available to practitioners during consultations for informed decision-making.

## Personal History

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Allergies** | Known allergies to medications, food, or environmental factors |
| **Medication History** | Past and current medications |
| **Habits** | Tobacco use, alcohol consumption, and other lifestyle factors |

## Medical History

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Past Medical Conditions** | Diabetes, Hypertension, Asthma, Heart Disease, etc. |
| **Chronic Illnesses** | Ongoing conditions requiring continued management |
| **Previous Diagnoses** | History of diagnoses from past encounters |

## Surgical History

Record previous surgeries with details:
- **Surgery name and type**
- **Date of surgery**
- **Hospital/facility where performed**
- **Outcome and notes**

## Family History

Capture hereditary and genetic risk information:
- **Condition** (e.g., Diabetes, Heart Disease, Cancer)
- **Relation** (Father, Mother, Sibling, etc.)
- **Age of onset** (if known)

## Risk Factors

Document patient-specific risk factors that influence care decisions:
- **Lifestyle risks** — Smoking, sedentary lifestyle, obesity
- **Occupational hazards** — Exposure to chemicals, physical strain
- **Genetic predispositions** — Based on family history
