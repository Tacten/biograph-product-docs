# Creating an Encounter

## From an Appointment

The most common way to create an encounter:

1. Open a **Patient Appointment** with status "Scheduled" or "Checked In"
2. Click the **Create > Patient Encounter** button
3. The encounter auto-populates:
   - Patient details
   - Practitioner information
   - Appointment reference
   - Department
4. Complete the clinical documentation and **Submit**

## Standalone Encounter

For walk-in patients or situations where an appointment wasn't booked:

1. Go to **Patient Encounter** list
2. Click **+ Add Patient Encounter**
3. Select the **Patient** and **Practitioner**
4. Fill in the clinical details and **Submit**

## Inpatient Encounter

For patients admitted to the facility:

1. Open the **Inpatient Record**
2. Click **Create > Patient Encounter**
3. The encounter is linked to the inpatient stay
4. Multiple encounters can be created during a single admission (daily rounds, specialist consultations)
